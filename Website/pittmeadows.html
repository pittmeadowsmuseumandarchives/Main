Pitt Meadows Day

Pitt Meadows Day is a family event that is made possible through the hard work of the Pitt Meadows Day Society and Volunteers. The event originally started as a Strawberry Festival during the Great Depression and grew from there and has now been celebrated for more than 70 years.

Celebrate Pitt Meadows Day with the Museum by visiting our two sites and enjoy our exhibits and special events programming!

Date: 1st Saturday of June
Time: Pitt Meadows Day events run all day starting at 7:00am and running to 10:00pm when there are fireworks. At the Museum our hours are 11:00 am through 3:00 pm.

Pitt Meadows Museum General Store, and The Hoffman & Son garage will be open during the afternoon for the public display starting at 11:00am, and will include an open Candy Store and gift shop, as well as live blacksmithing.
